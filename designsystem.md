# Design System: Vision 2020 Military Accessories Catalog

## 1. Brand Identity
* [cite_start]**Company Name:** Vision 2020[cite: 1, 7].
* [cite_start]**Background:** Over 20 years of experience in personal safety and body armor[cite: 9].
* [cite_start]**Certifications to Highlight:** ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, ISO 45001:2018[cite: 14].
* **Vibe:** Professional, secure, tactical, and robust.

## 2. Color Palette (Suggested)
* **Primary:** #1A202C (Tactical Black) - For headers, primary buttons, and footers.
* **Secondary:** #4A5568 (Gunmetal Gray) - For secondary text, borders, and subtle backgrounds.
* **Accent:** #4C514A (Olive Drab / Military Green) - For badges, highlights, and active states.
* **Background:** #F7FAFC (Light Gray/White) - To ensure high contrast and readability for product specs.
* [cite_start]**Warning/Alert:** #C53030 (Crimson Red) - For important notices (e.g., "Handle With Care")[cite: 257].

## 3. Typography
* **Headings:** `Oswald` or `Roboto Condensed` (Uppercase, bold, tactical feel).
* **Body Text:** `Inter` or `Roboto` (Clean, highly readable for technical specifications).

## 4. Core Components
* **Product Card:** * Image thumbnail (top).
    * [cite_start]Product Title & Model Number (e.g., "PASGT Boltless (S-L) | Model: MICH")[cite: 177, 190].
    * [cite_start]Quick Specs: Ballistic Material [cite: 191] [cite_start]& Protection Level[cite: 193].
    * "View Details" button.
* [cite_start]**Specs Table:** Clean, striped-row tables for technical variations (e.g., Hard Armor Plates weights and dimensions)[cite: 251, 263].
* [cite_start]**Feature List:** Bulleted lists with checkmark icons for product features[cite: 60, 61, 62].
